About Us


Welcome to Coop@Cafe — your favourite spot to unwind, sip, and connect right here on the Unisel Bestari Jaya campus.

Established three years ago, Coop@Cafe was born from a simple idea: to give students, staff, and visitors a cozy and welcoming space to enjoy a great drink without ever having to leave campus. What started as a humble campus café has grown into a beloved daily destination for the entire Unisel community.

We take pride in crafting every cup with care, offering a thoughtfully curated selection of refreshing beverages to suit every mood and moment — whether you need a morning boost before class, a midday refresher, or a relaxing drink to wind down after a long day.

At Coop@Cafe, we believe that a good drink has the power to bring people together. More than just a café, we are a place where friendships are made, ideas are shared, and memories are created — one sip at a time.

Come visit us. Your perfect drink is waiting.
